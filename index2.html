For Those Who Know...

GetUp - Rise to your feet… Get together… Get on down… Dance!.

TheGet UP is a collaboration of DJs Marshall, Paul Murray, Good2Groove and Chris Iredale. Start new sentence: “All reigning from Yorkshire with house in their blood, music has united them. Their shared ethos is getting together to host nights that make you move, by mixing up their unique music styles, DJ talents and club experience to create events true to the roots of House Music”. TheGet UP is the real deal - all about feeling the sound… ‘we want to get down and party with you.’

Our DJs

We have amassed some of the best resident DJs in the North of England. Marshall and Paul Murray are GetUp residents from Classic Leeds night ‘UpYerRonson’. Add to this the dynamic duo ‘Good2Groove’, who are regulars on the Northern house and disco scene. ‘Rare Beats’ devotee and dedicated vinyl ‘picker, Chris Iredale, completes the pack mixing choice cuts and rare selections to really move the crowd.

Along with residents we’ve had fantastic guests joining us, such as Tom Wainright who got the crowd going with his unique blend of beats. We’ve also got Full Intention and Terry Farley lined up to treat your ears later in the year.
